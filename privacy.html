Chat Analysis — Privacy Policy

Effective date: August 13, 2025  •  Version: 1.0

Thank you for using the Chat Analysis application. Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it when you use our application.

Information We Collect

Chat Analysis processes the following data to provide deep analysis of your WhatsApp conversations:

  • WhatsApp Chat Data: When you choose to analyze your WhatsApp chats using our application, this chat data is processed. Important: This data is processed only on your device and is not transferred to our servers.
  • Device Information: Basic information such as device model, operating system version, and application version for functionality and crash reporting.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous statistics about how the application is used (e.g., which features are used, application performance).

How We Use Your Information

  • To operate our application and provide WhatsApp chat analysis to you
  • To monitor and improve application performance
  • To detect and resolve technical issues
  • To improve our application and prioritize new features

Data Security

  • Local Processing: Your WhatsApp chat data is processed on your device and is not sent or stored on our servers.
  • Data Encryption: All data processed within the application is encrypted.
  • Minimal Data Collection: We collect only data that is necessary to provide our services.

Third-Party Services

Our application may use the following third-party services to function:

  • Analytics Services: We may use analytics services such as Google Analytics for Firebase to understand how our application is used. These services collect only anonymous usage data.
  • Crash Reporting: We may use crash reporting services (e.g., Firebase Crashlytics) to improve application stability.
